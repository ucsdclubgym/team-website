Fundraising

UCSD Club Gymnastics is composed of athletes with all different backgrounds and experience levels. Despite everyone’s different gymnastics journeys, we have been able to come together as a team and bond over this shared passion, and we are excited to take this into our competitions. Our team has been working hard to learn new skills and perfect routines in hopes of performing well this season. Everyone is getting excited to attend competitions and show off their hard work!
